# Product Requirements Document (PRD)

## Objective
Improve account activation time and lifecycle visibility while maintaining regulatory compliance.

## Problem Statement
Account lifecycle processes are fragmented, leading to delays, manual effort, and audit risk.

## In Scope
- Savings and current accounts
- Lifecycle status tracking
- Dormancy and reactivation flows
- Customer-initiated updates

## Out of Scope
- Core banking replacement
- Credit products

## Functional Requirements
- Unified lifecycle status engine
- Rule-based validations
- Event-driven notifications

## Non-Functional Requirements
- Auditability
- Reliability
- Backward compatibility

## Dependencies
- Core banking APIs (T24)
- KYC services
